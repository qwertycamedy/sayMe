SayMe – what is it?

SayMe offers a novel approach to anonymity. On an Instagram story, users share their SayMe link, allowing their followers to send them private messages to their SayMe mailbox. We think anonymity should offer a fun, secure environment where you may share your thoughts and ideas without fear of ridicule or condemnation. The current social media landscape leaves young people without a place to express their emotions without fear of judgment from peers or cultural expectations. For teens, SayMe offers this secure environment.

Why do my pupils use SayMe?

The authenticity of social media has drastically declined. Though the media first served as a platform for people to openly communicate their ideas, feelings, and lives, it has since evolved into a competition to see whose life can appear to be the most flawless. SayMe seeks to remove this appearance of untruthfulness and replace it with the freedom of anonymity, enabling our users to speak freely and openly without worrying about criticism. If your pupils are using SayMe, we can assure you that it is a new way for them to express themselves and is not simply another social media app taking up space on their phone.

Is it safe?

Team SayMe places a high priority on the security of the SayMe community. We even have a whole page devoted to ensuring your safety while using SayMe! We are cautious, and thus we take a number of precautions to protect our users, including:

1. To remove offensive language and bullying, we employ cutting-edge AI content moderation that makes use of deep learning and character pattern-matching algorithms based on rules. Additionally, our technology can determine the emojis' semantic meaning, and our web scraper collects specific instances of contextual emoji usage. As a result, we are up-to-date with culture, conversant in jargon, and skilled at spotting misleading information.

2. To protect our users, the SayMe community is subject to strict rules outlined in our detailed Community Guidelines, Terms of Service, and Privacy Policy. Please contact our team at safety@saymeapp.com or use the app's «Report» feature to report any offensive messages.

How can I defend my pupils who employ SayMe?

We value the safety of your students just as much as you do. We therefore offer a variety of safety precautions you can use with your students, including:

1. Blocking and reporting within the app. For you and your pupils to alert SayMe to dangerous messages, the SayMe app includes a Report function for each message you receive. Additionally, you can block persons on SayMe by choosing «Block» after reporting a message for «Bullying or harassment».

2. Speaking with SayMe. Team SayMe takes user reports and communications seriously, and thus we always keep an eye out for them. If you have any concerns about the security or well-being of your pupils, you can contact us at safety@saymeapp.com

What can I do about my kids using the word SayMe?

We at SayMe are fully aware that mastering the latest social media nuances and trends can be challenging because the world of technology can be a learning curve. We advise connecting with your pupils directly if you need assistance learning more about SayMe because frequently they can provide you the precise response you need! Please don't hesitate to contact our team at hello@saymeapp.com if you have any additional queries that need to be clarified. We will be happy to help.
